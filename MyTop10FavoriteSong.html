My Top 10 Favorite Songs

Little Dark Age

Little Dark Age is the fourth studio album by American rock band MGMT, released on February 9, 2018, via Columbia Records. 8 It was the band's first album of new material in more than four years, following the release of their eponymous third studio album in September 2013.

My Ordinary Life

Little Dark Age, abbreviated LDA, is the fourth studio album by American rock band MGMT, released on February 9, 2018, via Columbia Records. 8 It marked the band's first release of new material in more than four years, and was issued after the band's third studio album, titled the same name as the band itself, which was released in September 2013.

After Dark

After Dark, Mr. Kitty (2014) Synthwave The song quickly became a fan favorite that has accrued more than 100 million streams on Spotify. Its dark and mysterious lyrics have inspired many interpretations of its meaning. The song has a very dark and mysterious quality that is undeniable and has intrigued listeners all over the globe.

Wutiwant

"Wutiwant" is a song by the artist saraunh0ly, released in 2023 as part of the album "wutiwant (Sped Up & Slowed)." The track delves into themes of self-identity, emotional disconnection, and the quest for personal fulfillment.

Government Hooker

"Government Hooker" is a song by American singer Lady Gagafrom her second studio album, Born This Way (2011). The song was co-written and co-produced by Gaga, Fernando Garibay and DJ White Shadow, with additional production from DJ Snake. “Government Hooker” was an unused demo track Shadow made with DJ Snake. Sessions were recorded in 2010 at the Studio at the Palms in Las Vegas, Nevada.

Killer

"Killer" is a haunting track by Mareux, released in 2022 as part of the album Lovers From The Past. The song blends darkwave and synth-pop elements to create a moody atmosphere that complements its exploration of obsession, longing, and emotional turmoil.

Memory Reboot

VØJ and Narvent collaborated to create the Synthwave masterpiece "Memory Reboot." The song, which combined aspects of Cyberpunk and Synthwave, went viral on Tik Tok and swept the globe. Each producer contributes a distinct background.

Under Your Spell

“Under Your Spell” is a song by the American electronic duo Snow Strippers, from their 2023 album, April Mixtape 3. The song details the effects of emotional entanglement and the fettering aftereffects of a controlling partner. The song itself is electronic and dance, which leads into a dramatic and melancholy atmosphere.

Washing Machine Heart

"Washing Machine Heart" is an American song from the singer Mitski from her album Be the Cowboy, released in 2018. Released as not a single, but one of her most famous songs The single reached number 93 on the Irish Singles Chart and number 26 on the UK Indie charts.

The Lost Soul Down

NBSPLV, “The Lost Soul Down” (Legwork) Unfurled in a spar bare track built around the persistent repetition of the phrase “You love me, me,” this one is hypnotic and meditative. It is not too far-fetched to say that this repeats itself out of desperation for some type of assurance, love, or affection, reinforcing the notion of the complicated and cyclical nature of relationships and the constant search for approval.
